  <div class="group-summary">
    <p><strong>Princeton HAIKU Lab</strong> studies how humans and AI systems collaborate in complex decision-making environments.</p>
    <p>We use applied modeling, statistical evaluation and experimentation, combined with normative and sociotechnical analysis to understand and improve human-AI collaboration in consequential domains. Our goal is to design, evaluate, and guide the implementation of AI systems that advance societal and planetary outcomes for all.</p>
